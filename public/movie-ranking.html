The Best Movies According to Tatyana

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Lackawanna Blues

This my favorite black movie. Black joy, the role of nannies, and the racial lifestyle differences of the 1940s.

Fried Green Tomatoes

The movies shows aging, domestic abuse, family, lesbianism, gender inequality, and the impact of racism on both black and white people.

Crash

Tackles the sensitive subject of racism head-on by intertwining the stories of a litany of people from both sides of the proverbial tracks.
